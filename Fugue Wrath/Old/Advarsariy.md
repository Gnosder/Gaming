### Base Values by HD

Round up

AC = 11+(1/2)HD
ATK = 9 + HD
    If att greater 15, gain second attack, greater than 15 gain a 3rd
DMG = 2.5 x HD
SHK = 1/3HD / 10+HD
ML (Higher is better)
    Untrained Mortal  7
    Trained Mortal 8
    Veterain Mortal 9
    Simple Animal 6
    Predator 7
    Hulking Predator 10
    Automaton 12
    Mindless Beast 11
INST (Lower is better)
    Mortal 4
    Animal 6
    Monster 3-5
SKILL = 1/3 HD
SAVE = 9 + 1/2HD
